F.A.Q.

How to play?

After converting your selected song, press "Start." Use (ASDF) and hit the notes as they reach the bottom of the screen to score points. To play again, press "Restart." Ensure that volume is turned on.

Why MIDI files?

Musical Instrument Digital Interface (MIDI) files are formatted to include instructions about instruments, notes, and timing. To capture the characteristics and aesthetics of Chiptune music, audio waveforms of the musical tracks embedded in the MIDI file are manipulated to produce square, triangle, and sawtooth sound waves.

For a library to find and listen to MIDI files, visit freemidi.org.
